Hello, I'm Pranay Meshram

Researcher | Computer Vision Enthusiast | AI/ML Developer

I'm Pranay, a 3rd-year UG student at the Indian Institute of Science Education and Research (IISER) Bhopal, with a deep and growing interest in Computer Vision and Machine Learning. My current research involves using Vision Language Models for segmentation tasks and control in Autonomous Navigation at MuSiC Lab, IISER Bhopal.

I'm also working on Visual Place Recognition (VPR) and image geolocalization at IIT Roorkee under the guidance of Dr. Gaurav Kumar Nayak. Over the past year, I have worked on various projects, including building models from scratch like PaliGemma, ViT, and Transformers, fine-tuning LLMs like LLaMA 2 using LoRA and quantization, and deploying real-time ML models on Raspberry Pi using thermal imagery.
